Mistakes beginners make

Here are the errors we see repeatedly. Knowing them in advance saves both time and money.

Over-leveraging

Using too much leverage turns a small loss into a catastrophic one. We teach position sizing first.

Ignoring risk

Many beginners focus only on entry points. We teach that exit strategy and risk control define profitability.

Chasing hype

Social media trends are not strategies. We teach how to evaluate setups independently of noise.
