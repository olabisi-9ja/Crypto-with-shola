About Shola

Real name. Real story. Real mission.

Shola started in crypto not as an expert, but as someone frustrated by the noise. He built Crypto With Shola to teach people how to trade with discipline — not hype.

Before crypto education, Shola worked in traditional finance and technology. The transition taught him that success in trading is less about finding the perfect strategy and more about building the right habits. That insight became the core of everything here.

Background and transition

In 2019, Shola began sharing his trading journey publicly. What started as a personal blog quickly turned into a community. People did not just want signals — they wanted understanding. By 2021, the Telegram group had grown past a thousand members, and the education platform was born.

Today, Crypto With Shola operates as a multi-layered business: free education for beginners, premium memberships for active traders, and private mentorship for serious students. Every layer exists to serve a different stage of the journey.

His values

  • ● Transparency first. Show wins and losses. No edited screenshots.
  • ● Education over hype. The goal is independent traders, not followers.
  • ● Discipline over luck. Long-term profitability comes from process, not predictions.
  • ● Real identity. No anonymous accounts. Shola stands behind every word.

Why he teaches

The crypto space is full of promises and short on structure. Shola teaches because he believes discipline and education are the only sustainable advantages — and that anyone willing to learn can build real skill in this market.
